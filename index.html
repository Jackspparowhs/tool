How it works

Everything runs locally in your browser. We sample pixels from your image, cluster them with k-means, and show the most common colors. No image leaves your device.

Tips

  • Use lower k for simpler palettes (2–6).
  • Click an area in the image to add an exact pixel color to the palette.
  • If your image is very large, the sampler downsamples for speed.
