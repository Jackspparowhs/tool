Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the typing test?

Our test uses industry-standard WPM calculation (5 characters = 1 word) with real-time accuracy tracking, providing results within 1% margin of error.

Is my data stored or tracked?

No. All tests run entirely in your browser using localStorage. We don't store any personal data or typing history on our servers. Everything stays private and local.

Can I use this on mobile devices?

Yes! The test is fully responsive and optimized for touch keyboards on iOS and Android devices. However, physical keyboards provide the best experience for accuracy.
